StemSave

Stem cell preservation from teeth

StemSave focuses on preserving stem cells from eligible extracted teeth, including baby teeth, wisdom teeth, and selected healthy permanent teeth. Stem cells may have future potential in regenerative medicine. Ask us before an extraction if this may be an option.

StemSave explainer

Dental stem cell banking may preserve an option for future medical advances. If a tooth is being extracted, ask our doctors whether it may be eligible for StemSave collection.
